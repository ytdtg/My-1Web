Mt. Yangbew

Brief Description:

Mt. Yangbew is an ideal destination for tourists seeking a quick mountain escape near Baguio City. Known for its wide-open grassland summit and stunning sunrise views, it offers a relatively easy climb that takes most visitors only 30 to 45 minutes to complete. From the top, you can enjoy a full panoramic view of the La Trinidad strawberry farms and a frequent "sea of clouds" during the early morning hours. It is particularly popular for horseback riding and photography, providing a scenic backdrop that feels much more remote than its actual location.
