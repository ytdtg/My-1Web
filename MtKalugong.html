Mt. Kalugong

Brief Description:

Mt. Kalugong, located in La Trinidad, Benguet, is a serene eco-park and cultural village best known for its limestone rock formations that resemble a hat—hence the name “Kalugong,” which means hat in the local dialect. Mt. Kalugongoffers visitors panoramic views of the La Trinidad Valley, including its famous strawberry farms and pine-covered hills. The eco-park features picnic areas, hiking trails, and a cultural village that highlights Cordilleran heritage, making it both a nature escape and a cultural experience. With its cool mountain air, scenic vistas, and peaceful atmosphere.
