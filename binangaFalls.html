Binanga Falls

Brief Description:

Binanga Falls is a captivating natural attraction in Barangay Shilan, La Trinidad, known for its single-layer drop and its remarkably deep, jar-shaped catch basin called a "banga." Located downstream from Payogpog Falls, it is nestled among lush lettuce and sayote plantations and is a popular, albeit rugged, spot for cliff jumping and nature photography. Tourists can reach the falls via a short 15-minute downhill trek starting near Tacdian Elementary School, making it an easy side trip for those exploring the gardens of Shilan. While there is no official entrance fee, visitors are encouraged to coordinate with local officials for safety, especially if planning to swim in the 20-meter-deep waters.
