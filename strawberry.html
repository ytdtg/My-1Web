Strawberry Farm

Brief Description:

La Trinidad Strawberry Farm in Benguet is a well‑known agritourism destination just outside Baguio City, famous for its wide fields of strawberries where visitors can enjoy pick‑your‑own harvesting. Operated by Benguet State University and local farmers, the farm covers dozens of hectares and offers fresh strawberries alongside other local produce. With its cool mountain climate, scenic surroundings, and hands‑on experience of picking ripe berries straight from the plants, the farm has become a must‑visit spot for tourists and a vital source of livelihood for the community.
