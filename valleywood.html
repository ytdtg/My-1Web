Valleywood

Brief Description:

Valleywood Adventure Park is a scenic eco-tourism spot in Barangay Tawang, La Trinidad, famous for its massive "VALLEYWOOD" sign and panoramic views of the valley. Formerly known as Rocky Mountain, the park features pine-covered trails and creative photo spots like the "To the Moon" sculpture, making it popular for both sunrise treks and evening night-viewing. It is highly accessible from Baguio City and offers amenities such as a cafe and overnight camping for an entrance fee of approximately ₱130(Price may CHANGE).
