Valley Of Colors

Brief Description:

The Valley of Colors in La Trinidad, Benguet is a striking hillside community where hundreds of clustered homes have been painted in bold, vibrant hues, creating a massive mural visible from Halsema Highway. Officially called “Colors of StoBoSa,” it covers the sitios of Stonehill, Botiwtiw, and Sadjap, and was launched in 2016 as a tourism project inspired by South Korea’s Gamcheon Cultural Village. Once a plain hillside, it is now a lively landmark that symbolizes community spirit and creativity, attracting visitors for its colorful scenery, photo opportunities.
