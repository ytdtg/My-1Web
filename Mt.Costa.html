MT. Costa

Brief Description:

Mt. Costa is a sprawling 6-hectare garden park in La Trinidad, Benguet, often referred to as the "Green Living Room" for its serene and artistic atmosphere. Unlike a traditional hiking peak, it features 24 uniquely themed gardens connected by scenic walking trails, including the popular Mirror Garden and the interactive Musical Garden. It is a prime destination for tourists who enjoy photography and leisurely strolls, as the park beautifully blends diverse botanical collections with creative art installations. Located about 20 to 30 minutes from Baguio City, it offers a peaceful retreat where visitors can explore two main trails—the Yellow Trail for garden enthusiasts and the Blue Trail for those seeking art—before relaxing at the on-site cafe.
