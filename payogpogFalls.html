Payogpog Falls

Brief Description:

Payogpog Falls is a scenic 30-foot cascade tucked away in Barangay Shilan, La Trinidad, offering a refreshing and less-crowded alternative to the typical tourist spots near Baguio. Often reached through the private Sagpawe’s Hidden Garden, the falls are part of a lush river system that includes a series of small natural pools perfect for a quick dip in the cold mountain water. To get there, visitors typically undergo a short but slightly rugged 10 to 15-minute trek from the garden, which is itself a paradise for plant lovers and succulent collectors. Because it is located in a quiet, rural area, it provides a peaceful atmosphere for nature photography and picnics, especially when combined with a visit to the nearby flower farms and gardens in the Shilan area.
